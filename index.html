Dr. Maryam Mirzahkani

Fields Medalist

Dr. Mirzahkani shown in a profile by the New York Times. Image © Stanford University
Maryam Mirzahkani was born on May 12, 1977 in Tehran, Iran. Showing early promise in mathematics, in 1994 she won gold at the International Mathematical Olympiad in Hong Kong, the first Iranian woman to do so. In 1995 she followed her first gold with a second gold, and a perfect score, at the International Mathematical Olympiad in Toronto.
